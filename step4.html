📌 Step 4: Find a Small Warehouse or Storage Unit – Rent Space for Raw Materials & Finished Products

📍 Location: Karur, Tamil Nadu, India

👤 Role: Managing Director -

📌 Objective: Secure a cost-effective, strategically located warehouse or storage unit for Natseer (Y-Diet Project) to efficiently store raw materials, packaging, and finished products while ensuring optimal inventory management.

🔹 List of Needs (Requirements for Warehouse Storage)

✅ Warehouse Space Requirements

  • Storage Area: Minimum 300-1000 sq. ft. (Expandable if demand increases).
  • Temperature Control: Ensure proper ventilation & humidity control for food storage.
  • Separate Sections:
    • Raw Materials Storage (Grains, herbs, nuts, spices).
    • Packaging Materials Storage (Eco-friendly pouches, labels).
    • Finished Goods Storage (Packed Y-Diet products, ready for sale).

✅ Warehouse Location Preference

  • Near manufacturing unit for quick access.
  • Close to transportation hubs (Karur Bypass, Coimbatore Road).
  • Accessible to logistics & delivery services.

✅ Warehouse Facilities Needed

  • Electricity & Power Backup (For lighting, small processing).
  • Water Supply (For cleaning & basic processing).
  • Security Measures (CCTV, locks, guards if necessary).
  • Loading/Unloading Area (Space for small trucks & delivery vans).

✅ Legal Considerations

  • Rental Lease Agreement (6-12 months minimum).
  • Clear rental terms (deposit, monthly rent, maintenance responsibility).
  • Fire & Safety Compliance (Storage approval for food products).

✅ Estimated Budget for Warehouse Rent & Setup

Expense Type Estimated Cost (INR) Remarks
Warehouse Rent (300-1000 sq. ft.) ₹10,000 - ₹30,000/month Based on size & location
Security Deposit (2-3 months) ₹20,000 - ₹60,000 Negotiable with landlord
Electricity & Water Setup ₹5,000 - ₹10,000 Basic installation
Storage Racks & Shelving ₹10,000 - ₹25,000 For inventory management
CCTV & Security ₹10,000 - ₹20,000 Optional, for large warehouses
Total Estimated Budget ₹55,000 - ₹1,45,000

🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Finding & Renting a Warehouse

1️⃣ Define Warehouse Storage Requirements

  • 🔹 Identify the required space based on the volume of raw materials & finished products.
  • 🔹 Choose a location close to manufacturing & suppliers to reduce transport costs.
  • 🔹 List must-have facilities (power, water, ventilation, security).

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Clear understanding of space & infrastructure needs.

2️⃣ Search & Shortlist Warehouse Spaces

  • 🔹 Look for rental spaces via:
    • Local Property Dealers & Brokers in Karur.
    • Online Listings (99Acres, MagicBricks, OLX, Facebook Marketplace).
    • Industrial Warehouses Near Karur Bypass & Coimbatore Road.
  • 🔹 Compare shortlisted locations based on:
    • ✅ Rental price & lease terms.
    • ✅ Proximity to suppliers, manufacturing unit, & transportation hubs.
    • ✅ Storage conditions (humidity, ventilation, safety).
    • ✅ Flexibility for expansion (if needed in future).

🎯 Outcome: ✅ 3-5 best warehouse options selected for inspection.

3️⃣ Visit & Inspect Warehouse Locations

  • 🔹 Check the condition of the warehouse:
    • ✅ Ventilation & humidity control (important for food products).
    • ✅ Power & water supply availability.
    • ✅ Accessibility for delivery vehicles.
    • ✅ Security arrangements (locks, fencing, CCTV).
  • 🔹 Discuss with the landlord:
    • ✅ Rent & security deposit.
    • ✅ Maintenance responsibility.
    • ✅ Rental agreement flexibility (short-term vs. long-term).

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Finalize the best warehouse space.

4️⃣ Negotiate & Finalize Lease Agreement

  • 🔹 Negotiate:
    • Lower deposit amount (if possible).
    • Better maintenance terms (landlord should handle basic repairs).
    • Rent-free period (some landlords offer 1-2 months free to set up).
  • 🔹 Ensure the lease agreement includes:
    • Rental amount & duration.
    • Exit clause (for terminating lease early).
    • Electricity & maintenance responsibilities.
    • Permission to modify space (install storage racks, CCTV, etc.).

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Warehouse legally rented with clear terms.

5️⃣ Set Up Warehouse for Storage & Operations

  • 🔹 Basic Setup:
    • ✅ Install shelving & racks for raw materials & finished products.
    • ✅ Set up labeling & batch tracking system.
    • ✅ Arrange inventory sections (Raw Materials, Packaging, Finished Goods).
  • 🔹 Security & Maintenance:
    • ✅ Install CCTV cameras & safety measures.
    • ✅ Train staff on proper storage, handling & fire safety.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Warehouse ready for business operations.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

Issue Possible Solution
📍 High Warehouse Rent in Preferred Location Look for warehouses outside city center but close to transport hubs.
🔌 No Proper Ventilation or Power Supply Choose a warehouse with open airflow & backup power options.
📝 Landlord Demanding High Security Deposit Negotiate for lower deposit or flexible payment plan.
🚛 Difficult Vehicle Access for Loading/Unloading Ensure the warehouse has proper road access & parking space.
🏗️ No Space for Expansion in Future Choose a lease with an option to extend or shift to a larger unit later.

🎯 Final Deliverables

  • ✅ Warehouse Rented & Lease Agreement Signed
  • ✅ Storage Sections Organized (Raw Materials, Finished Goods, Packaging)
  • ✅ Security & Maintenance Systems in Place
  • ✅ Inventory Management System Ready for Operations
