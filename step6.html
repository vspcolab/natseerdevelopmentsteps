📍 Location: Karur, Tamil Nadu, India

👤 Role: Managing Director -

📌 Objective: Hire or onboard a small, efficient team to handle daily operations for Natseer (Y-Diet Project) within a limited budget and workspace.

🔹 List of Needs (Requirements for Hiring Staff)

✅ Roles & Responsibilities (Minimum 1-3 Team Members Needed Initially)

Role Key Responsibilities Skills Required Estimated Salary (INR/Month)
Operations Manager Oversee daily activities, supplier coordination, inventory management Supply Chain, Vendor Management ₹15,000 - ₹30,000
Production & Packing Assistant Handle food processing, packaging, labeling, and quality control Basic Food Handling, Packaging ₹10,000 - ₹20,000
Sales & Marketing Executive Manage online sales, customer queries, and social media marketing Digital Marketing, Customer Service ₹12,000 - ₹25,000

✅ Hiring Criteria & Selection Process

  • Experience: Preferably 1-3 years in food, retail, or e-commerce.
  • Flexibility: Willing to multi-task in a startup environment.
  • Local Hiring Preference: Reduces commute & salary expectations.
  • Part-Time/Freelance Model: If full-time hiring is costly, start with freelancers for marketing.

🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Hiring a Small Team

1️⃣ Identify Key Roles & Responsibilities

  • 🔹 List the essential tasks for daily operations (e.g., supplier coordination, packaging, online sales).
  • 🔹 Define which roles can be handled by a single person (e.g., one person can manage both sales & marketing).
  • 🔹 Prioritize multi-skilled candidates to reduce initial hiring costs.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Clear job roles & responsibilities outlined.

2️⃣ Choose Hiring Methods & Platforms

  • 🔹 Free & Low-Cost Hiring Platforms:
    • LinkedIn & Naukri.com (Professional Hiring).
    • Facebook Job Groups (Local Hiring).
    • WhatsApp & Local Job Agencies (For Packing & Warehouse Staff).
    • Freelancer Websites (Fiverr, Upwork – For Marketing & Digital Work).
  • 🔹 Referral-Based Hiring:
    • Ask existing business contacts & suppliers for recommendations.
    • Hire experienced workers from competitor brands (if possible).

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Shortlist 5-10 potential candidates.

3️⃣ Conduct Interviews & Select the Right Candidates

  • 🔹 For Operations Manager:
    • Ask about previous experience in supply chain, logistics, and vendor management.
    • Test problem-solving skills (handling inventory shortages, supplier delays).
  • 🔹 For Production & Packing Assistant:
    • Assess knowledge of food safety, handling, and packaging speed.
  • 🔹 For Sales & Marketing Executive:
    • Test social media skills (running ads, handling customer complaints).
    • Ask for past sales or customer service experience.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Select 1-3 employees based on budget & priority.

4️⃣ Finalize Salary, Contracts & Onboarding Process

  • 🔹 Offer competitive salaries with performance incentives.
  • 🔹 Prepare a simple employment contract covering:
    • Work hours & responsibilities.
    • Payment schedule & incentives.
    • Probation period (2-3 months trial before full-time hiring).
  • 🔹 Provide basic training on Y-Diet products, packaging, and sales.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ New team members legally onboarded & trained.

5️⃣ Set Up a Basic Work Environment & Employee Tools

  • 🔹 Arrange:
    • ✅ Desk, chairs, and computers for admin work.
    • ✅ Weighing machines & sealing machines for packaging.
    • ✅ POS system (if selling offline), invoicing tools (Tally, Zoho Books).
  • 🔹 Create a simple daily workflow for employees to follow.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Fully functional workspace for new hires.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

Issue Possible Solution
📍 High Salary Expectations from Candidates Start with part-time/freelancers, offer performance-based incentives.
📝 Lack of Skilled Workers in Karur Expand job search to nearby districts & flexible remote options.
🚀 Employee Performance Below Expectations Implement trial period (2-3 months), training sessions.
💰 Limited Budget for Hiring Full-Time Staff Hire 1-2 key employees first, outsource remaining tasks.
🏗️ Workload Too High for a Small Team Use automation tools (n8n for task management, WhatsApp Business for customer queries).
