📌 Step 3: Obtain Export Certifications – Register with APEDA & Other Food Safety Authorities

📍 Location: Karur, Tamil Nadu, India

👤 Role: Managing Director -

📌 Objective: Obtain the necessary export certifications to legally sell Y-Diet products internationally, ensuring compliance with Indian & foreign food safety regulations.

🔹 List of Needs (Requirements for Export Certification)

✅ Essential Export Certifications for Food Products:

  • APEDA (Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority) – Mandatory for exporting food products.
  • FSSAI Central License – Required for food processing and export.
  • Import Export Code (IEC) – Compulsory for international trade.
  • Certificate of Origin (COO) – Confirms that products are made in India for trade benefits.
  • US FDA Registration (If Exporting to the USA) – Compliance for US food import regulations.
  • HALAL Certification (For Middle East Markets) – Needed for exports to UAE, Saudi Arabia, etc.
  • EU Organic Certification (If Selling in Europe) – Required for organic food exports.

✅ Basic Documents Needed:

  • Business Registration Documents (GST, MSME, FSSAI License).
  • PAN & Aadhaar of Owner.
  • Rental Agreement (Office/Warehouse).
  • List of Products for Export.
  • Bank Certificate or Canceled Cheque.

🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Export Certification Registration

1️⃣ Apply for APEDA Registration (Export Authorization)

  • 🔹 Go to APEDA Official Website.
  • 🔹 Click on Register as Exporter → Fill in Business Details.
  • 🔹 Upload GST, PAN, FSSAI License, MSME Registration, Import Export Code (IEC).
  • 🔹 Pay ₹5,900 registration fee online.
  • 🔹 APEDA certificate is issued within 10-15 days.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Business officially recognized as an exporter of agricultural & processed food.

2️⃣ Obtain FSSAI Central License (For Exporters)

  • 🔹 Go to FSSAI Portal.
  • 🔹 Select Central License (for exports and large-scale manufacturing).
  • 🔹 Upload:
    • Manufacturing unit details.
    • List of food products.
    • Safety & hygiene compliance documents.
  • 🔹 Pay registration fees based on turnover (₹7,500 - ₹25,000).
  • 🔹 Inspection may be conducted by an FSSAI officer.
  • 🔹 Receive FSSAI Central License within 30 days.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Business legally approved for food manufacturing & export.

3️⃣ Apply for Import Export Code (IEC)

  • 🔹 Go to DGFT IEC Portal.
  • 🔹 Fill in Business Name, PAN, GSTIN, and Address Proof.
  • 🔹 Pay ₹500 processing fee and submit application.
  • 🔹 IEC code is issued within 5-10 days.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Business legally authorized to trade internationally.

4️⃣ Obtain Certificate of Origin (COO)

  • 🔹 Apply via:
    • APEDA for agricultural & food products.
    • FIEO (Federation of Indian Export Organisations).
    • Local Chamber of Commerce.
  • 🔹 Submit:
    • Business registration proof.
    • Export invoice & packing list.
    • Bank certification.
  • 🔹 Processing time: 2-3 days.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Proof of Indian origin for Natseer (Y-Diet) products, enabling trade benefits.

5️⃣ Apply for US FDA Registration (If Exporting to the USA)

  • 🔹 Go to US FDA Registration Portal.
  • 🔹 Submit:
    • FSSAI License.
    • HACCP (Food Safety Certification).
    • Details of Food Products.
  • 🔹 Pay fees (₹15,000 - ₹30,000).
  • 🔹 Registration approval in 4-6 weeks.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Products legally approved for export to the USA.

6️⃣ Apply for HALAL Certification (For Middle East Exports)

  • 🔹 Apply via:
    • HALAL India (halalindia.co.in).
    • JAKIM (Malaysia), ESMA (UAE), SFDA (Saudi Arabia).
  • 🔹 Submit:
    • Product composition details.
    • Processing & packaging compliance.
  • 🔹 Inspection & audit may be required.
  • 🔹 Approval takes 4-6 weeks.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Certification ensures compliance with Islamic dietary laws for exports to Gulf countries.

7️⃣ Apply for EU Organic Certification (If Selling in Europe)

  • 🔹 Apply via APEDA (for NPOP – National Programme for Organic Production).
  • 🔹 Submit:
    • Organic farm & processing details.
    • Ingredient sourcing verification.
  • 🔹 Certification cost: ₹50,000 - ₹1,00,000.
  • 🔹 Approval time: 8-12 weeks.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Certification enables organic product sales in Europe (EU), UK, and other international markets.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

Issue Possible Solution
📍 Delay in APEDA Registration Approval Ensure all documents are uploaded correctly, and follow up via APEDA helpdesk.
🔌 FSSAI Central License Rejected Due to Missing Details Provide clear product descriptions & packaging compliance details.
📝 IEC Registration Rejected Due to PAN/GST Mismatch Verify that PAN & GST details are the same across all applications.
🚀 HALAL Certification Taking Too Long Choose a certification body accredited by GCC (Gulf Cooperation Council) for faster approval.
🌍 EU Organic Certification Cost Too High Apply for government subsidies or phased certification for different product batches.
