Step 1 - Find & Rent Office Space in Karur (Natseer Y-Diet Project)

📍 Location: Karur, Tamil Nadu, India

👤 Role: Managing Director - Srithik

📌 Objective: Secure a cost-effective, strategically located office space for Natseer (Y-Diet Project) with limited budget, space, and staff to support business operations.

🔹 List of Needs (Requirements for Office Space)

✅ Budget:

  • Monthly Rent: ₹5,000 - ₹15,000
  • Security Deposit: 2-3 months advance (₹10,000 - ₹30,000)
  • Setup Cost (Furniture, Internet, Branding): ₹10,000 - ₹20,000
  • Total Budget: ₹25,000 - ₹65,000

✅ Ideal Office Location:

  • Near transport hubs & supplier networks (Gandhi Puram, Karur Bypass, Coimbatore Road).
  • Easily accessible by logistics & staff.
  • Safe, with reliable power supply & internet connectivity.

✅ Space Requirements:

  • Minimum 100-300 sq. ft.
  • Basic amenities: Electricity, water, restroom, security.
  • Meeting space for supplier discussions.
  • Storage area for documents & small product samples.

✅ Essential Infrastructure:

  • Reliable Internet Connection (Airtel, Jio, BSNL).
  • Furniture (Desk, chairs, storage racks).
  • Printer, scanner, and basic office supplies.

✅ Legal Considerations:

  • Short-term lease (6-12 months) with an option to extend.
  • Clear rental agreement (no hidden charges or unfair clauses).
  • Check for rent escalation & exit clause.

🔹 Step-by-Step Execution Plan

1️⃣ Define Office Space Requirements

  • 🔹 Finalize budget, space needs, location preferences, and lease duration.
  • 🔹 Identify key office usage (admin, meetings, inventory tracking, digital marketing).
  • 🔹 Set aside funds for security deposit, utilities, and setup costs.

2️⃣ Search & Shortlist Available Office Spaces

  • 🔹 Look for options via:
    • Online Listings (99Acres, MagicBricks, OLX, Facebook Marketplace).
    • Local real estate agents & brokers in Karur.
    • Co-working spaces or shared offices (for cost-saving).
  • 🔹 Compare shortlisted locations based on:
    • ✅ Rental cost vs. budget.
    • ✅ Proximity to suppliers, transport, and market access.
    • ✅ Facilities available (power backup, internet speed, security).
    • ✅ Future expansion possibility (option to add more space if needed).

3️⃣ Visit & Evaluate Shortlisted Office Spaces

  • 🔹 Visit the top 3-5 selected office spaces and check for:
    • ✅ Accessibility & location advantage.
    • ✅ Power supply, internet availability, parking.
    • ✅ Condition of the building & maintenance quality.
    • ✅ Additional charges (electricity, water, maintenance fees).
  • 🔹 Take photos & compare all options before finalizing.

4️⃣ Negotiate & Finalize the Lease Agreement

  • 🔹 Negotiate terms with the landlord:
    • ✅ Monthly Rent & Security Deposit.
    • ✅ Maintenance responsibilities (who pays for repairs?).
    • ✅ Agreement duration & rent escalation conditions.
    • ✅ Exit clause (minimum notice period for moving out).
  • 🔹 Ensure legal agreement includes:
    • ✅ Clear rental terms & conditions.
    • ✅ Landlord’s responsibilities (basic repairs, utilities).
    • ✅ Advance payment receipt & legal stamp paper agreement.

5️⃣ Office Setup & Initial Operations

  • 🔹 Set Up the Office:
    • ✅ Install Wi-Fi, phone lines, & electricity connections.
    • ✅ Arrange furniture (desks, chairs, storage units).
    • ✅ Set up printer, scanner, computers, and digital tools.
    • ✅ Add branding elements (Natseer signage, logo, office decor).
  • 🔹 Basic Operations to Start:
    • ✅ Register office address for business communication.
    • ✅ Enable supplier meetings & order coordination.
    • ✅ Begin daily operations, record-keeping, & documentation.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

Issue Solution
📍 High Rent in Preferred Location Look for spaces outside the city center but well-connected.
🔌 No Proper Electricity or Internet Confirm availability with electricity board & internet providers before signing lease.
📝 Landlord Asking for High Security Deposit Negotiate for lower deposit or installment-based payments.
🚗 Poor Transport Access Choose an office near highways & public transport routes.
💧 Lack of Basic Facilities (Water, Parking, Restroom) Ensure these are included in lease agreement before finalizing.

🎯 Final Deliverables

  • ✅ Office Space Rented & Legally Registered
  • ✅ Lease Agreement Signed with Clear Terms
  • ✅ Infrastructure Setup (Furniture, Internet, Power)
  • ✅ Business Ready for Operations
