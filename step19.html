📌 Step 19: Create Product Catalog & Marketing Materials – Develop Brochures, Digital Assets, and Content for Promotion

📍 Location: Karur, Tamil Nadu, India

👤 Role: Managing Director -

📌 Objective: Design and develop a professional product catalog, brochures, and digital marketing materials to promote Natseer (Y-Diet) products effectively across online and offline channels.

Section Details to Include

🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Creating Product Catalog & Marketing Materials

1️⃣ Develop the Product Catalog (Print & Digital Versions)

  • ✅ Structure the Catalog for Maximum Impact.
  • ✅ Cover Page with Brand Logo, Tagline, High-Quality Product Image.
  • ✅ Detailed Company Introduction & Vision Statement.
  • ✅ Categorized Product Listings with Features, Benefits, and Pricing.
  • ✅ Include FSSAI, Organic, APEDA, and other Certifications.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ A professional PDF & print-ready product catalog is created.

2️⃣ Create Engaging Brochures & Flyers for Offline Marketing

  • ✅ Trifold Brochures for retail and distributor marketing.
  • ✅ Single-Page Flyers for events, exhibitions, and awareness campaigns.
  • ✅ Infographic Flyers showing nutrition facts, benefits, and testimonials.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Ready-to-print brochures & flyers for offline distribution.

3️⃣ Design Social Media & Website Marketing Graphics

  • ✅ Instagram & Facebook: Regular posts, carousel ads, reels.
  • ✅ LinkedIn: Professional product announcements.
  • ✅ YouTube & TikTok: Short video ads & testimonials.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Consistent brand presence on social media with engaging content.

4️⃣ Produce High-Quality Product Demo Videos

  • ✅ How-to-Use Videos: Explain how to consume/use the product.
  • ✅ Customer Testimonials: Real people talking about product benefits.
  • ✅ Founder’s Message: Building brand connection & trust.
  • ✅ Animated Infographics: Nutritional benefits, health advantages.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Engaging video content ready for promotions.

5️⃣ Optimize E-Commerce Listings & Website Content

  • ✅ Optimize Listings for SEO.
  • ✅ Write engaging product descriptions with benefits and use cases.
  • ✅ Add high-quality product images for better click-through rates.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ SEO-optimized product listings for higher online sales.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

Issue Possible Solution
📍 High Cost of Graphic Designers Start with Canva/DIY designs, upgrade later.
📝 Marketing Content Not Engaging Use real customer stories & before/after results.
🚀 Social Media Posts Not Getting Views Run targeted Facebook & Instagram ads.
💰 Printing Costs Too Expensive Print only in bulk, use PDFs for digital sharing.
🏗️ Low Clicks on E-Commerce Listings Improve product descriptions, add lifestyle images.
