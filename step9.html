🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Creating a Branding Kit

1️⃣ Design the Company Logo

  • 🔹 Decide on Brand Identity & Theme:
    • ✅ Colors (Natural, Earthy, Green, Gold for Premium)
    • ✅ Style (Minimalist, Organic, Traditional Ayurvedic Look)
    • ✅ Font Style (Bold, Readable, Clean)
  • 🔹 Use Online Tools or Hire a Designer:
    • Free Tools: Canva, Looka
    • Professional: Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW
    • Freelance Platforms: Fiverr, Upwork

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Unique, high-quality logo finalized.

2️⃣ Create Product Labels & Packaging Design

  • 🔹 Key Details on Product Labels:
    • ✅ Brand Name & Logo
    • ✅ Product Name & Description
    • ✅ Nutritional Facts & Ingredients
    • ✅ FSSAI Number & Manufacturing Details
    • ✅ Barcode & Expiry Date
  • 🔹 Use Label Design Software or Hire Experts:
    • DIY: Canva, Photoshop, CorelDRAW
    • Professional Services: Freelancer, Printify
  • 🔹 Print & Test Labels Before Finalizing

🎯 Outcome: ✅ FSSAI-compliant, visually appealing product labels ready.

3️⃣ Design the Website Layout (E-Commerce Ready)

  • 🔹 Choose a Website Platform:
    • For Simplicity: Wix, WordPress
    • For E-Commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce
    • For Custom UI/UX: Figma → Web Developer
  • 🔹 Must-Have Pages:
    • ✅ Home Page: Company introduction, core values
    • ✅ Product Page: Display meal kits, functional foods
    • ✅ Blog Section: Articles on health, diet, wellness
    • ✅ About Us & Contact Page
  • 🔹 Ensure SEO & Mobile-Friendly Design is Crucial

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Website structure & layout designed for launch.

4️⃣ Set Up Social Media Profiles & Brand Identity

  • 🔹 Create Accounts for Natseer on:
    • ✅ Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter
    • ✅ YouTube (For Video Content & Product Demos)
  • 🔹 Use Consistent Branding:
    • ✅ Profile & Cover Images with Logo
    • ✅ Color-Coordinated Graphics & Templates
    • ✅ Brand Hashtags (e.g., #NatseerHealth, #YDiet)

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Brand presence established on key platforms.

5️⃣ Develop Marketing Materials & Brochures

  • 🔹 What to Include in a Brochure:
    • ✅ Introduction to Natseer (Y-Diet Project)
    • ✅ Product Benefits & Features
    • ✅ Testimonials & Certifications
    • ✅ Contact Information & Website
  • 🔹 Design Tools:
    • ✅ Canva (Simple & Free)
    • ✅ Adobe InDesign (Professional Brochures)

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Ready-to-print and digital brochure for marketing.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

Issue Possible Solution
📍 Logo Not Looking Professional Hire a graphic designer from Fiverr or Upwork.
📝 Product Labels Not Compliant with FSSAI Cross-check mandatory details with FSSAI guidelines.
🚀 Website Looks Basic & Unprofessional Use Shopify/Wix premium templates or hire a UI/UX designer.
💰 High Cost of Branding Start with DIY tools (Canva, Wix Free Plan), upgrade later.
🏗️ Social Media Pages Not Growing Invest in ads & influencer collaborations to build presence.
