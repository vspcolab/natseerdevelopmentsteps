Step 2: Register Natseer as a Legal Business – Obtain MSME, GST, and FSSAI Licenses

📍 Location: Karur, Tamil Nadu, India

👤 Role: Managing Director -

📌 Objective: Legally register Natseer (Y-Diet Project) as a business entity, ensuring compliance with Indian laws for domestic & export sales.

🔹 List of Needs (Requirements for Legal Registration)

✅ Business Structure Selection:

  • Proprietorship (Ideal for small-scale businesses, easy to register)
  • Partnership (If multiple owners are involved)
  • Private Limited Company (Pvt Ltd) (For future scalability & investment)

✅ Key Registrations Required:

  • MSME Registration (Udyam Registration) – For government benefits & subsidies.
  • GST Registration – Mandatory if annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakh (₹20 lakh for service-based).
  • FSSAI License (Food Safety) – Compulsory for manufacturing, packaging, and selling food products.
  • Import Export Code (IEC) (if exporting) – Required for international trade.

✅ Basic Documents Needed:

  • Aadhar Card & PAN Card (Owner)
  • Rental Agreement or Utility Bill (Office Address Proof)
  • Bank Account in Business Name
  • Product Details (for FSSAI registration)

🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Legal Registration

1️⃣ Register MSME (Udyam Registration)

  • 🔹 Go to Udyam Registration Portal.
  • 🔹 Enter Aadhar & PAN details of the business owner.
  • 🔹 Fill in business name, type (Manufacturing – Food Processing), office address, and bank details.
  • 🔹 Submit & get MSME certificate (Udyam Registration Number) instantly.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Business officially recognized as MSME & eligible for govt. benefits.

2️⃣ Apply for GST Registration

  • 🔹 Go to GST Portal.
  • 🔹 Select New Registration → Proprietorship/Company.
  • 🔹 Upload Aadhar, PAN, Address Proof, Business Details.
  • 🔹 Enter HSN Code for Y-Diet Products:
    • Cereal & Health Mixes – 1901
    • Herbal Supplements – 3004
  • 🔹 Receive GSTIN (GST Number) within 7-10 days.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ GST number obtained, eligible for input tax credit & legal invoicing.

3️⃣ Apply for FSSAI Food License

  • 🔹 Go to FSSAI Portal.
  • 🔹 Select Basic (Local Sales) or State (If Selling in Multiple States) or Central (Exports & Large Sales).
  • 🔹 Upload Food Business Details, Owner's ID Proof, Address Proof, Product Description.
  • 🔹 Pay Fees & Submit Application.
  • 🔹 FSSAI inspector may visit for verification (for State & Central Licenses).
  • 🔹 Receive FSSAI License Number (valid for 1-5 years).

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Business legally authorized to manufacture & sell Y-Diet products.

4️⃣ Obtain Import Export Code (IEC) (If Exporting)

  • 🔹 Go to DGFT IEC Portal.
  • 🔹 Fill in Business Name, PAN, GSTIN, and Address Proof.
  • 🔹 Pay ₹500 processing fee.
  • 🔹 Receive IEC Code via email within 5-10 days.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Business legally authorized to export products internationally.
