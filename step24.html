🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Supplier Evaluation & Quality Control

1️⃣ Develop Supplier Evaluation Framework

  • ✅ Identify critical quality parameters for raw materials & packaging.
  • ✅ Assign weightage to different evaluation criteria (price, quality, delivery).
  • ✅ Develop supplier scoring system (1-5 scale for each factor).

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Clear supplier assessment framework in place.

2️⃣ Conduct Supplier Audits & Sample Testing

  • ✅ Visit supplier facilities (if possible) to inspect production process.
  • ✅ Request sample batches for laboratory testing (FSSAI, APEDA, USDA).
  • ✅ Compare quality, moisture content, shelf-life, and purity of samples.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Reliable suppliers shortlisted based on quality & compliance.

3️⃣ Implement Supplier Performance Monitoring System

  • ✅ Track order accuracy & batch rejection rate (<2%).
  • ✅ Monitor timely deliveries (90%+ on-time rate).
  • ✅ Evaluate response time to issues & flexibility in supply terms.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Real-time tracking system for supplier reliability.

4️⃣ Negotiate Continuous Quality & Pricing Improvements

  • ✅ Request bulk order discounts (10-20% off for annual contracts).
  • ✅ Encourage suppliers to adopt better eco-friendly practices.
  • ✅ Develop a backup supplier strategy to avoid dependency.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Long-term supplier partnerships optimized for quality & cost.

5️⃣ Finalize Supplier Agreements & Compliance Protocols

  • ✅ Agreed product specifications & quality control measures.
  • ✅ Minimum acceptable quality levels (e.g., <2% defective rate).
  • ✅ Pricing, payment terms, and delivery schedules.
  • ✅ Return & refund policies for defective goods.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Supplier contracts finalized & quality compliance ensured.
