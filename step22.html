🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Supplier Contract Negotiation & Pricing

1️⃣ Prepare a Negotiation Plan with Target Pricing & Terms

  • ✅ Decide target price per ingredient (based on market rates & profit margins).
  • ✅ Choose preferred payment terms (e.g., 50% advance, Net 30 days, partial prepayment).
  • ✅ Identify backup suppliers to strengthen negotiation leverage.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Defined pricing targets & negotiation strategy.

2️⃣ Contact Suppliers & Initiate Pricing Discussions

  • ✅ Call/email suppliers to request bulk pricing & MOQ details.
  • ✅ Compare pricing from multiple suppliers to find the best deal.
  • ✅ Highlight potential long-term partnerships to secure better rates.
  • ✅ If needed, visit supplier facilities to inspect production capabilities.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Multiple pricing quotes collected & compared.

3️⃣ Negotiate Bulk Discounts, Payment Terms & Delivery Conditions

  • ✅ Leverage Bulk Orders: "If we commit to monthly orders, can we get 15-20% off?"
  • ✅ Flexible Payment Terms: "We prefer Net 30 payment. Can you accommodate that?"
  • ✅ MOQ Reduction: "For the first order, can we start with 50% MOQ?"
  • ✅ Shipping & Logistics: "Can you offer free shipping for orders above ₹2,00,000?"

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Best pricing, favorable payment terms & reliable delivery conditions negotiated.

4️⃣ Finalize Supplier Contracts & Sign Agreement

  • ✅ Final agreed price per kg/unit for each raw material.
  • ✅ Payment schedule & mode (Bank transfer, credit terms).
  • ✅ Quality standards & rejection policy (Minimum accepted purity level).
  • ✅ Delivery schedule & penalties for delays.
  • ✅ Replacement & refund policy for defective batches.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Legally binding supplier agreements signed.

5️⃣ Place First Bulk Order & Monitor Supplier Performance

  • ✅ Place initial bulk order (small batch to test reliability).
  • ✅ Track delivery timelines & order fulfillment rate.
  • ✅ Test sample batch quality before full-scale production.
  • ✅ Set up ongoing supplier performance tracking.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ First bulk order placed & supplier relationship established.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

  • 📍 Supplier Unwilling to Offer Discounts: Offer long-term contracts for better rates.
  • 📝 High MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) Requirement: Negotiate lower MOQ for trial order before committing to larger batches.
  • 🚀 Payment Terms Not Favorable: Use staggered payments (e.g., 30% advance, 70% on delivery) to reduce cash flow risk.
  • 💰 Raw Material Costs Fluctuating: Lock in fixed pricing for 6-12 months to avoid market fluctuations.
  • 🏗️ Supplier Delays in Delivery: Add penalties for late shipments in contracts.
