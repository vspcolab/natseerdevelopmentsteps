🔹 Step-by-Step Procedure for Small-Scale Inventory & Market Testing

1️⃣ Produce Small-Batch Inventory for Market Launch

  • ✅ Manufacture limited batch (100-500 units per product).
  • ✅ Ensure proper packaging, labeling, and barcoding.
  • ✅ Assign batch numbers for quality tracking.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Ready-to-sell pilot inventory for testing.

2️⃣ Set Up Storage & Inventory Management System

  • ✅ Allocate storage space (cool, dry conditions for sensitive products).
  • ✅ Implement basic inventory tracking software (Google Sheets, Tally, Zoho).
  • ✅ Set minimum stock alerts to prevent shortages.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Organized inventory ready for efficient order fulfillment.

3️⃣ Launch Pilot Sales Across Selected Distribution Channels

  • ✅ List 10-20 units per product for controlled test sales (Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify).
  • ✅ Partner with 3-5 organic stores & health retailers.
  • ✅ Offer 1-month Meal Kit subscription for early adopters.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ First batch of products successfully launched in the market.

4️⃣ Monitor Key Performance Metrics & Adjust Strategy

  • ✅ Track sales conversion rates, customer feedback, and repeat purchase trends.
  • ✅ Offer introductory discounts or bundle deals if needed.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Data-driven insights collected for scaling the business.

5️⃣ Scale Up Based on Market Feedback & Demand Trends

  • ✅ Expand production from 500 units to 2000+ units.
  • ✅ Onboard more distributors & online retailers.
  • ✅ Optimize logistics & pricing based on real sales data.

🎯 Outcome: ✅ Full-scale production & distribution planned based on pilot sales success.

🔹 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

  • 📍 Low Sales from Online Store: Improve product descriptions, run ads, offer first-time discounts.
  • 📝 Retailers Hesitant to Stock Products: Provide free samples & promotional incentives.
  • 🚀 Delivery Delays Affecting Customer Experience: Partner with reliable logistics providers (Shiprocket, Delhivery, Bluedart).
  • 💰 Inventory Not Moving Fast Enough: Introduce bundle pricing & special limited-time offers.
  • 🏗️ Customer Complaints About Packaging/Taste: Adjust packaging design, refine product formula based on feedback.
